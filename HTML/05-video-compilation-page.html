Mitski,
Live From Roadrunner in Botson

Compilation of videos from Mitski's live performance at Roadrunner in Boston.

Francis Forever

Francis Forever is a poignant song that captures the essence of longing and nostalgia. Mitski's heartfelt lyrics and emotive delivery make it a standout track, resonating with anyone who has experienced deep emotional connections.

Nobody

Nobody by Mitski is a danceable yet emotional song about loneliness and longing. Behind its upbeat sound lies a deep sense of isolation, with the repeated “nobody” expressing both desperation and quiet resignation.

Townie

Townie is a powerful anthem that speaks to the struggles of feeling out of place. Mitski's raw and honest lyrics, combined with her unique sound, create a compelling narrative about identity and belonging.
